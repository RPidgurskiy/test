DNIPRO-PARTNER LLC has been working on the Ukrainian market for over 20 years now. We work in the field of transport services and the specific equipment services (including special agricultural equipment). DNIPRO-PARTNER is also one of the most dynamic trading companies in the agricultural sector of Ukraine.
Today, the company has confidently established itself as a reliable partner for a large number of regular customers (in the total customer base of more than 5,000 counterparties).
The main activities of the company are:
  • transportation of liquid cargo by tank trucks (including those that require a special permit);
  • transportation of the large-sized cargoes (including specific equipment);
  • transportation of grain crops and their processing products;
  • services of agricultural equipment for cultivation, fertilizer application, etc.;
  • trade of grains, seeds, plant protecting tools, agricultural products (including export operations).

Due to the presence of an extensive car park network and places of permanent deployment of equipment, the territory of DNIPRO-PARTNER services includes all the regions of Ukraine.

Also, with great encouragement, we join the international cooperation and provide transportation services on individual requests and routes.

DNIPRO-PARTNER respects each client and tries not only to provide services but also to create a real partnership for the long term!

So, we are always happy to see the new clients, because the new victories inevitably will be waiting for us together with them!
